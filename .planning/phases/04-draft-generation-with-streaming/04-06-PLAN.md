<diagnosed_gap>
- UAT result: day labels are missing and meals are rendered in rows by day instead of columns by day.
- Root cause: MealPlanGrid currently renders `buildExpectedSlots()` into a flat `grid-cols-1 sm:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3` card grid, so there is no structural place for day headers or meal-type row alignment.
</diagnosed_gap>

<ui_contract>
- Desktop: days are columns, meal types are rows.
- Mobile: each day is rendered as a vertical section with a visible day heading.
- Existing SSE slot mapping remains keyed by `day_of_week` + `meal_type`; only the presentation structure changes.
</ui_contract>
</context>
